About CityGo

The goal of CityGo is to document and promote local events in an accessible way, supporting community organizers and local artists to reach their audiences.

Our Vision

A vibrant city — culturally and socially active — where every event can find its audience.

Event Publication Policies (Summary)

To maintain a fair and community-oriented environment, all submitted events must follow the guidelines below.

  • Public or Clearly Defined Events

    Events should be open to the public or include clearly stated participation conditions.

  • No Political or Unauthorized Commercial Content

    Political party content or unapproved commercial advertising is not permitted.

  • Provide Clear Event Details

    Each event must include a clear description, date, and location for approval.
